About Moon Fox Farm:


Moon Fox Farm is located on 5 acres in the cow spotted hills between Petaluma and Point Reyes. The land was previously farmed organically for 9 years. It has been farmed using biodynamic practices since 2012 and is in the process of becoming certified. Dedicated to growing open-pollinated, diversified crops in harmony with natural rhythms, Moon Fox Farm offers a wide variety of specialty heirlooms from around the world, strengthening and preserving our great culinary heritage.


Farmer Annie Izaki has a passion for food and the beauty of nature. With years of experience working on several organic farms in Vermont and across Northern California as well as studying permaculture strategies and completing a biodynamic apprenticeship under Harald Hoven at Rudolph Steiner College, Farmer Izaki started Moon Fox Farm in hopes of getting people excited about roots, leaves, flowers, and fruit and to build on the connection between human, animal, plant, earth, water, and sky.
