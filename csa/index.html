The Moon Fox Farm CSA delivers food for the living - colorful and vital produce straight from the earth to you. Moon Fox is a biodynamic farm located in rural Marin County within an hour from the SF BAY. We harvest the same day as delivery, insuring not only the freshest but most nutritious food available. We grow a diverse selection of specialty heirloom varieties for superior flavor, beauty, and sustainability. Trade time shopping in a store for time cooking and eating at home. Support local sustainable agriculture for a healthier planet and a stronger local community. Know where the food came from and how it was grown. Connect to the land and its living virtues. Taste the difference, live the difference. Please email moonfoxfarm@gmail with any questions.
