The Moon Fox Farm CSA delivers food for the living - colorful and vital produce straight from the earth to you. FARM SHARE DELIVERIES TO PETALUMA, SF, AND THE EAST BAY.

How does it work?

You can receive farm shares every week or biweekly (every other week). CSA shares are packed in wax boxes and need a shady safe spot where they can be dropped off. We ask CSA members to return boxes (ideally we drop off a full one and pick up an empty). Delivery times vary depending on location, but Wednesday afternoon-evening is the typical window. Home deliveries are available to many locations in SF, El Cerrito, Albany, Berkeley, and Oakland. Petaluma locals can pick up a box at The Mail Depot. If you are interested in home delivery, email moonfoxfarm@gmail.com to confirm your address is on route.

What will you get in your box?

You will get a balance of color, nutrition, and flavors of the season. We grow as diverse a crop selection as we can while focusing on what people want to eat regularly. All our crops are heirlooms, many from seed we have saved ourselves, creating a richer, more unique flavor and sense of place.


Each box will contain a variety of lettuces and/or greens for all your salad and smoothie needs, and a mix of aliums (onions, garlic, leeks, shallots, scallions), root vegetables (carrots, beets, turnips, radishes, parsnips…), tubers (potatoes, sunchokes…), fruits (peas, melons, cucumbers, tomatoes, peppers, beans…), and herbs (cilantro, parsley, thyme, oregano, sage, lovage, chives…).


We listen to your comments and try to grow more of what people like and ask for. For example, we’re bringing back broccolini in 2015, loved and missed in 2014.



Payments

We love payments for the full 9‐month season in advance. It is part of the original CSA model and helps the farm get off to a good start. We are also offering a 3‐month payment option (3 payments per season). But we don't want to discourage those with a smaller cash flow. If you are interested and excited about the CSA and would like to pay monthly or with EBT benefits, please email moonfoxfarm@gmail.com.
