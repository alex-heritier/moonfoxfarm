2016 CSA RUNS MAY THROUGH NOVEMBER

You can receive farm shares every week or biweekly (every other week). Moon Fox Farm specializes in growing heirloom varieties and unique vegetables, fruits, grains, and flowers. You most likely will try something new and splendid. All crops are harvested just before delivery to ensure the freshest and most nutritious produce you can find anywhere.


We deliver to the East Bay and SF area in the evenings from approx. 7—9pm. This keeps the produce out of the hot sun and the driver out of the hot traffic. If you are home evenings or have a safe spot to leave a CSA box and are interested in home delivery, please email moonfoxfarm@gmail.com to confirm your address is within our route before you make a payment.
